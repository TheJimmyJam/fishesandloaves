Our Mission

A community coming together to feed its own.

Fishes & Loaves Emergency Food Pantry exists to make sure that no one in our community goes without food. Inspired by the miracle of generosity — where a little becomes enough for everyone — we believe in showing up for our neighbors.

Every Saturday morning, our volunteers gather to distribute free groceries, canned goods, produce, and household staples to anyone who walks through our doors. No income verification, no lengthy forms. Just real help for real people.

We are a faith-based community resource open to everyone regardless of background, faith, or circumstance.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We probably have an answer below.

Who can use the food pantry?+
Anyone in need is welcome. Fishes & Loaves is open to all community members regardless of income level, background, or faith. If you need food, we're here for you — no questions asked.
Do I need to make an appointment or register?+
No appointment necessary and no registration required. Simply show up during our Saturday hours (10 AM – 12 PM) and our volunteers will assist you.
What documents or ID do I need to bring?+
We ask for a photo ID just once a year. Each January we re-register everyone, and anyone visiting for the first time after January will show ID once at that visit. After that, you're logged in our system and won't need to show ID again for the rest of the year — we'll simply look you up. We also use ID to note the number of people in your household so we can make sure you're getting the right amount of food for your family.
What kinds of food are available?+
Our inventory varies week to week based on donations, but we typically have canned goods, dry staples (pasta, rice, beans), fresh produce when available, bread, and other non-perishables.
How often can I visit?+
You're welcome to visit every Saturday. We don't limit how often you come — if you need ongoing food support, we want to be here for you.
Is there a cost?+
Everything we provide is 100% free of charge. We are donation-supported and never charge for food assistance.
Can I bring my kids?+
Absolutely. Families are always welcome. Kids are part of our community too.
How can I donate food?+
We accept food donations any day except Saturday — Saturday is our distribution day when we're focused on serving families. To donate, just give us a call to schedule a drop-off or, for large donations, we'll arrange a driver to come pick it up. Most-needed items: canned proteins (tuna, chicken, beans), pasta, rice, cooking oil, and peanut butter.
Can I volunteer?+
Yes! We always need helping hands on Saturday mornings. Reach out through the contact form and we'll get you plugged in.
Are you affiliated with a church?+
Fishes & Loaves is faith-inspired but serves everyone regardless of religion or beliefs. You won't be required to attend any services to receive assistance.
