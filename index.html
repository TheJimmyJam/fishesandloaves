Our Mission

A community coming together to feed its own.

Fishes & Loaves Emergency Food Pantry exists to make sure that no one in our community goes without food. Inspired by the miracle of generosity — where a little becomes enough for everyone — we believe in showing up for our neighbors.

Every Saturday morning, our volunteers gather to distribute free groceries, canned goods, produce, and household staples to anyone who walks through our doors. No income verification, no lengthy forms. Just real help for real people.

We are a faith-based community resource open to everyone regardless of background, faith, or circumstance.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We probably have an answer below. If not, reach out directly.

Who can use the food pantry? +
Anyone in need is welcome. Fishes & Loaves is open to all community members regardless of income level, background, or faith. If you need food, we're here for you — no questions asked.
Do I need to make an appointment or register? +
No appointment necessary and no registration required. Simply show up during our Saturday hours (10 AM – 12 PM) and our volunteers will assist you.
What documents or ID do I need to bring? +
You don't need to bring any documents or identification. We don't require proof of income, residency, or anything else. Just come as you are.
What kinds of food are available? +
Our inventory varies week to week based on donations, but we typically have canned goods, dry staples (pasta, rice, beans), fresh produce when available, bread, and other non-perishable items. We do our best to offer variety and nutrition.
How often can I visit? +
You're welcome to visit every Saturday. We don't limit how often you come — if you need food support on an ongoing basis, we want to be here for you.
Is there a cost? +
Everything we provide is 100% free of charge. We are a donation-supported ministry and never charge for food assistance.
Can I bring my kids? +
Absolutely. Families are always welcome. Kids are part of our community too, and we love seeing families come together.
How can I donate food? +
We gratefully accept non-perishable food donations. You can drop off donations at our location on Saturday mornings. Most-needed items include canned proteins (tuna, chicken, beans), pasta, rice, cooking oil, and peanut butter. Please check expiration dates before donating.
Can I volunteer? +
Yes! We always need helping hands on Saturday mornings. Volunteering is a great way to give back and get involved in the community. Reach out through our contact section and we'll get you plugged in.
Are you affiliated with a church? +
Fishes & Loaves is a faith-inspired food pantry, but we serve everyone in our community regardless of religion or beliefs. You won't be required to attend any services or programs to receive assistance.
